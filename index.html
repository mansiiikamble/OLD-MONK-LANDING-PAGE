No Gimmicks. Just the Monk.

ICONIC AND SMOOTH

Old Monk is more than a dark rum; it is a cultural cornerstone launched in 1855, carrying over a century and a half of history in every sip. Produced using a guarded, traditional recipe, its smooth, unmistakable flavor has remained a dependable constant for generations of loyal patrons. This unwavering commitment to tradition is what solidifies its status, proving that true quality and legacy require no advertising, only unwavering trust.

The Design is a Time Capsule.

Old Monk's bottle design has remained largely unchanged since its inception, reflecting the brand's commitment to tradition and timelessness.

The classic amber glass bottle, adorned with a simple yet elegant label featuring the iconic Old Monk logo, evokes a sense of nostalgia and authenticity. This enduring design not only pays homage to the brand's rich heritage but also reinforces its identity as a trusted and beloved rum that has stood the test of time.

AMBER

Matured for less time than the original, Old Monk Amber offers a lighter body and a subtly sweeter, vanilla-caramel flavor ideal for versatile mixing.

COFFEE

This variant is a dark rum infused with coffee, delivering a rich, bittersweet profile that is perfect for dessert cocktails or a unique digestif.

WHITE

This clear rum is either un-aged or aged very briefly, providing a crisp, clean base perfect for classic light mixers and fruit-forward cocktails.

#1 RECIPE

Funky Monk at Bonobo

Bonobo has some spectacular cocktails, but we find ourselves going back time and again for the Funky Monk. The coffee and sugar syrup in this drink gives it that extra kick that helps you keep going all night as the in-house DJ knocks out the slick beats that this Bandra bar is known for. Better still, it’s incredibly easy to make yourself too!

#2 RECIPE

Rum Café Gola at Woodside Inn

The Rum café gola is the perfect solution for when life throws you curveballs, merging the carefree simplicity of youth with the strong necessities of adulthood. It features the nostalgic, hard-packed ice that takes you back to your childhood, but delivers a strong, caffeinated rum kick that reminds you that you're an adult who has to show up to work tomorrow.

#3 RECIPE

Lick My Peach at Bonobo

If you’ve never had a peach cocktail, you’re really missing out. It’s fruity enough to make you think you’re having a light drink, but when the alcohol kicks in (which it inevitably does) it’s a not-so-subtle punch. Naturally, we love it. Especially if the peach drink in question is Lick My Peach.
